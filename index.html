About Aetas

We are a contrarian hedge fund focused on value-driven equities balanced with 'long volatility' type strategies to boost portfolio performance in all market cycles. We believe proper diversification combined with value investment based decision making leads to outperformance over the long run. Therefore, the prudent investor should be long quality equities, long bonds/credit, long/short commodities and long volatility, with different weightings at different points in the market cycle. Machine learning and deep learning algorithmic systems aid us in our decision making, however, most investment decisions are discretionary as we are medium to long term investors.

Our fund is founded and led by Daniel S. Bassett in his capacity as Chief Investment Officer. Mr. Bassett has been an active investor in both public and private securities for over 6 years. He started out mining and trading cryptocurrencies (far before anyone knew about them), before making his way into more 'traditional' forms of investing in commercial real estate, stocks and bonds. He received degrees in Economics and Philosophy from the University of Texas at Austin.
